Modes of Association

The means of association that we are willing to offer to your potential consumers follows a certain scheme as of now. Details of the scheme is given below: The consumer would buy in a stake at our company for BDT 30,000 initially. A separate account would be created under the name of the consumer, and a credit amount of BDT 30,000 would be debited to the consumer’s account. The credit amount would be valid for the following two years. As a consumer puts in an order for a certain or a personalized product, half of the product value would be adjusted from the credit amount, while the remaining half would have to be paid by the consumer. This would allow the consumers to pay a one-time subscription fee for the service, and the best part is that the subscription fee would again be utilized to provide further benefit to the consumer.
