How We Started

365 is an amalgamation of technology with the conventions of tailoring. This is a service product that we are offering to you with the aim of making your life easier. With the advancement in modern technologies, the rates of smartphone and internet penetration has multiplied quite significantly over the years. By daunt of which, we have obtained various services such as Pathao, Uber etc. making our lives easier. 365 is such a service, that is aimed at making your life much easier and offering you superior values.

At 365 A, we are aiming to offer you the services of conventional tailoring through digital platforms, with a blend of personalization. We are aiming to offer our customers their own design lab in order to design their attire to their desire. Anyone can sign up for the website, and get access to the design lab to design the fit, finish, print, texture and many more elements of the attire to their desire.
