How We Started

The value proposition for 365 A is “Offering the best, and personalized dress for our consumers at the best value”. Our dresses will provide the best fit and finish for the consumers, and the best part is we are willing to offer the service at a price much cheaper than the brand shops. Along with that, since we will be offering a design lab to our consumers, our consumer would be able to upload their own designs and tailor their product in accordance to that. This would allow our products to be unique and no two customer will have the same variant of the offered product.

Order Process

We will launch a website under the domain www.365A.com; where the customers can sign up and log in to their accounts to input their detailed information. The customers can then choose to design their own product through the design lab, or they may choose their liked product from our pre-designed list of products under different categories. The customer would be able to add their desired products in the cart under their account. In accordance to their added product, a support team would contact the customer within the shortest possible time, and upon confirmation, a design team would be sent to the customer’s address. The design team would show the various elements of the product to be tailored, for instance, the fabric to be used, the buttons, and the design patterns and so on. This step is to ensure that our

Customers get the best possible product, and that they get a product that exactly meets up to their expectation. Upon confirmation by the design team, the order would be processed and the finished product will be delivered to the customer’s residence or delivery address within the shortest possible time (minimum 6 working days). For any issues regarding the product, the customer can call the helpline to issue a service request, and a representative from the company would reach for help to the customer at the shortest possible time. All these steps are to ensure that the consumer has the best possible service from our end.

365 Delivery

We are also willing to offer an added service for our valuable customers under the name of 365 Delivery. Under this service, the customers can choose to order products from our website and have the product delivered overseas within the shortest possible time.
